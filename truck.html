Truck Track App

Truck Track is an app created for a prodcut design class at Parsons school of design. Mobile restaurants are hard to keep up with, Truck Track makes it easier for food truck owners to keep their customers updated on their latests stops, menu, and upcoming events and business hours. Features include: The ability to lookup the nearest food trucks with directions, menu and reviews, live location updates, business hours, and user profile pages!
