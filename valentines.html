Truck Track

Truck Track is a food truck navigating app created for a class assignment. Mobil restaurants are hard to keep up with, Truck Track makes it easier for food truck owners to keep their customers updated on their latests stops, menu, and upcoming events and business hours.























Features

  • Lookup nearest food trucks with directions
  • View menu
  • Live updates from Food trucks via social media
  • Business hours
  • Create personal profile
  • Reviews
  • Save favorites
