Unofficial Committee

Overview

Unofficial Committee is a community of activists, designers, and artists who wish to make a positive social impact. We believe as artists and designers we can inform and empower people in our communities by giving them the right resources to push for social change.


As a contributing multi-disciplinary designer, I create a variety of design assets in many mediums depending on the project the committee is currently working on. Some examples include; Motion graphics, Social media stickers/posts, Infographics, Augmented Reality, and posters.


To learn more about what we do, and what current projects we are working on, check out our website at:
unofficialcommittee.com


2020 Elections


Young eligible voters make up the most underrepresented group in the US ballots. In the 2020 primary elections, only 13% of people between the age of 18-29 voted. Choices and actions that will have the most effect on younger generations are being decided by older voters. That is why UC wants to bring awareness to young voters to register to vote and take action. Social medias such as Instagram, are a powerful tool for advocacy and spreading awareness. In a time of a global pandemic, we want to spread information on how to vote including alternatives like mail-in ballots.
