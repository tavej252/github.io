Jissaura Taveras is a multi-disciplined designer from Providence, RI. She specializes in UX/UI and digital interactive media. She has an affinity for learning more about the ways humans interact with products to create meaningful interactions and storytelling. Her main interest includes using her creative skills to explore ways to make impactful changes.


Amidst a time where activism is prominent, she hopes to use her visions to spread awareness for social, political, and ecological issues. She is inspired by her personal upbringing, to someday make technology and art more accessible to unrepresented groups.


Jissaura’s aesthetic is inspired by the colorful world around her, she admires neon signs and bright vivid things. Her work reflects her love of color and big bold type that’s anything but boring.


Jissaura’s work aims to create a more open-minded, inclusive, and socially conscious world by starting conversations about the taboo.
